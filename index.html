Research Highlights

I'm dedicated to building general purpose embodied agents that could actively explore and interact with the 3D physical world. I like to think of this in terms of neuroscientist Anil Seth's five perspectives of selfhood:

①
②
  • Perspectival self (①): Building 3D foundation models and world models for perceiving and understanding the 3D physical world. 3D-LLM, 3D-CLR, 3D-CG etc.
  • Bodily self (②): Embodied reasoning for robots, acting through and reasoning about a physical body situated in 3D space. 3D-VLA, MultiPLY etc.
  • Volitional self (①↔②): Intention and agency, the sense of urges to act and of being the cause of what happens, closing the perception-action loop, how perception changes as a function of action. ESI-Bench, Test-Time Planning, Embodied Web Agents.
  • Narrative self (Emerged from countless perception-action ①↔② loops): Life, Experience and Long-Term Memory of a continuous and distinctive agent, accumulating a history of experience across episodes rather than starting fresh each time. SlowFast-VGen, 3DLLM-Mem etc. I cover more of this in my blog, Three Levels of TTT.
  • Social self (Parallel ①↔② loops): Refracted through the minds of others, shaped by our social surroundings. We have some ongoing and future works on this, stay tuned!
